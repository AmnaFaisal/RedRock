Our Mission
At Red Rock Construction, we are committed to excellence, providing the highest quality construction services in New York City. Our mission is to enhance the city's beauty and functionality with every project we undertake. Our diverse and talented team collaborates seamlessly to deliver exceptional results, ensuring timely completion and adherence to budget.
We take pride in our unwavering reliability, fostering a smooth and satisfying experience for our clients. Red Rock Construction is not just about creating structures; we're dedicated to building your dreams.

About Red Rock

Founded in 2013, Red Rock General Contracting has established itself as a premier provider of residential and commercial renovation services across all five boroughs of New York City. Our team is committed to delivering top-notch quality and excellence in every project. Red Rock is fully licensed and insured, holding certifications and licenses from the following:
  • Department of Buildings (DOB)
  • Department of Consumer Affairs (DCA)
  • General Contractor License
  • Scaffolding License
  • All OSHA Certificates
  • Boom Lift License
Our expertise and dedication to safety ensure that we meet the highest standards in the industry, making us a trusted partner for your renovation needs.
